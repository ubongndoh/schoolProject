NEW CREATION GROUP OF SCHOOLS

New Creation Group of schools started with lessons for little children of christ for the world misson in 1992 at 38 Abasi Obori Street Calabar. It was then translated into nursery and primary school in 1995.

In 2003, it obtained government approval for recognition to operate. Thereafter, the school relocated to its permanent site at 4 Uwanse Close, near UwansePolice Station.
