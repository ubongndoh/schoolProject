NEW CREATION GROUP OF SCHOOLS

New Creation Group of schools started with lessons for little children of christ for the world misson in 1992 at 38 Abasi Obori Street Calabar. It was then translated into nursery and primary school in 1995. In 2003, it obtained government approval for recognition to operate. Thereafter, the school relocated to its permanent site at 4 Uwanse Close, near UwansePolice Station.

Over the years, parents of graduands of the primary school began to request for the establishment of a secondary school to ensure continuity of the sound academic and moral discipline they saw in their children. The request was granted by the proprietor with a pilot group of students in 2018/2019 academic session.
In 2018/2019 school year, the high school commenced its full operation. Deacon Francis B. Onabe is the pioneer principal of the school. In September 2020, the school was granted approval and recognition to operate.
Later in the same year, the school got an approval to conduct the JSS3 and the SS2 Mock Examination. Early in 2021 the school got NECO approval for the junior and senior exams.

Our Mission

New Creation Group of schools is commited groom godly children for self reliance, effective and efficient society service at the local,national and international levels.

Our Vision

Creating a conducive environment, using top class facilities and human resources for academic, moral, religious and skill development of a total child.

Core Values

» Commitment

» Spiritual Development.
